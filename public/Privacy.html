Morpha Privacy Policy

Effective Date: September 1, 2025

Contact: privacy@getmorpha.com

1) Quick Summary

  • We collect only what's needed while your contract is being analyzed: a session identifier (a temporary technical marker such as your IP address combined with browser/device type) and session length.
  • Uploaded contracts are processed in your session only and not stored permanently.
  • Contract text is processed through a trusted third-party AI provider (currently OpenAI). OpenAI does not use API data to train its models. Logs may be retained for up to 30 days for safety/compliance purposes, then removed. For detailed reference links, see Point 4.
  • We do not sell your data.
  • No user accounts at this time; everything is session-based.

2) Information We Collect

We collect information only while your contract is being analyzed and reviewed during your session:

  • Session & technical data: IP address, browser/device details (user-agent), and session duration.
  • Document content you upload: The contract text you submit for analysis.

No other personal identifiers (such as names, emails, or payment data) are collected.

3) How We Use Information

We use the information we collect only to:

  • Provide contract analysis and plain-English summaries.
  • Highlight risks and red flags in your document.
  • Enable chat-based Q&A about your contract.
  • Monitor session length and number of sessions for basic service reliability.

That is the full and complete list — nothing else.

4) Third-Party AI Processing (currently OpenAI)

To provide analysis, we transmit uploaded text to a trusted third-party AI provider (currently OpenAI).

For full details, see:

  • OpenAI API Data Usage Policy
  • OpenAI "Your Data" (Platform Data Controls)

Key points from OpenAI's policies:

  • No model training: Data sent via the API is not used to train or improve OpenAI models unless you explicitly opt in.
  • Retention: OpenAI may retain API inputs/outputs for up to 30 days to monitor abuse and meet compliance obligations, then deletes them (unless legally required to retain longer).
  • Security & access: Data is encrypted in transit and at rest, with access restricted to authorized personnel.
  • Ownership: You retain ownership of your inputs and outputs.

If we change providers in the future, this section will be updated.

5) Our Retention

  • Uploaded contracts: Processed only during your active session; any temporary files are deleted within 24 hours.
  • Session metadata (aggregate): Retained briefly to maintain reliability and detect abuse, then purged.

6) Cookies & Local Storage

We may use strictly necessary cookies or browser storage to keep your document active during your session (e.g., maintaining state). We do not use advertising or tracking cookies. (We may update this section if cookie usage changes.)

7) Sharing & Selling

  • We do not sell or rent your data.
  • Data is shared only with OpenAI, for the purpose of analyzing your contract through their API. OpenAI may retain API inputs/outputs for up to 30 days for abuse monitoring and compliance, after which they are deleted unless legally required to keep longer.

8) Security

We apply industry-standard safeguards: encrypted transfers, restricted access, and safe coding practices. No system is perfectly secure. If a breach occurs, we will take appropriate steps and notify as required by law.

9) Your Rights

  • Our third-party provider (currently OpenAI) will not use your API data for model training.
  • OpenAI may keep your inputs/outputs for up to 30 days only for:
    1. Abuse monitoring,
    2. Service functionality, and
    3. Legal compliance.

Because Morpha does not maintain accounts or store your documents, there is generally no data to delete after your session ends. If you have concerns about retained metadata, contact privacy@getmorpha.com.

10) Children

Morpha is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from children.

11) Changes

We may update this policy as we add features (such as accounts or document storage). Updates will include a new effective date and clear notice of material changes.

12) Contact

Questions or requests: privacy@getmorpha.com
